# Competitor Analysis: Normastroy Pitch Deck

## 1. Layout Structure
* **Overall Composition:** Horizontal flow for processes; centered radial layouts for market data; dense tabular grid for business model.
* **Grid System:** Transitions from a single-column process map to a split-column market slide, then to a multi-row canvas.
* **Content Alignment:** Centered for market diagrams; mixed alignment for the business model canvas.
* **Section Hierarchy:** Process flow uses a top-down timeline; market slide uses a "zoom-out" concentric hierarchy.
* **Distribution of Visual Weight:** Focused on the center of the slide for diagrams; evenly distributed for the canvas slide.
* **Slide Balance:** Heavily centered in the first two slides; grid-locked in the third.

## 2. Spacing & Composition
* **Margin Consistency:** Narrower margins compared to Competitor 01, maximizing space for complex diagrams.
* **Padding between elements:** Tight in the business canvas; generous in the process timeline.
* **White Space Usage:** Used effectively in the TAM/SAM/SOM slide to emphasize the scale of the circles.
* **Content Breathing Room:** Variable; low in the "Business Model" slide, high in the "Process" slide.
* **Density of Information:** High; particularly in the last slide which functions as a "One-Pager" overview.

## 3. Typography
* **Font Style:** Cyrillic Sans-Serif.
* **Heading Hierarchy:** Bold, blue titles; smaller bold black sub-headers.
* **Font Weight Variations:** Multiple weights used to distinguish between titles, labels, and numbers.
* **Text Sizing:** Market numbers are significantly larger than the labels ("TAM", "SAM").
* **Line Spacing:** Tight (approx 1.2) to fit more data in the canvas sections.
* **Contrast Effectiveness:** Good; blue/black on white is standard and legible.

## 4. Color System
* **Primary Colors:** Royal Blue (#0052FF), White.
* **Accent Colors:** Green (success/checkmarks), Red (alerts/nodes), Sky Blue (Market circles).
* **Background Treatment:** Pure white for content areas; deep blue for the overall presentation "stage" (frame).
* **Contrast Strategy:** High contrast (Blue on White).
* **Visual Emphasis:** Red nodes on the timeline indicate specific milestones or checkpoints.
* **Brand Consistency:** Unified use of blue across all slide types.

## 5. Design Patterns
* **Repeating Visual Components:** Rounded "chips" or "tags" for categories in the business canvas.
* **Card Systems:** Not used; relies on borders and background shading for sectioning.
* **Section Dividers:** Thin horizontal lines for connecting data points; shaded backgrounds for canvas blocks.
* **UI Motifs:** Progress nodes (circles 1-7) on a wavy path.
* **Shape Usage:** Circles (nodes/market), Rounded rectangles (canvas blocks).

## 6. Visual Elements
* **Icons:** Simple outline icons (Check, Clock, Gear, User, Folder).
* **Illustrations:** Wavy line "Process Path"; Concentric "Market Circles".
* **Graphics:** BIM360 and proprietary logos used as trust signals.
* **Data Visualizations:** Radial market chart (TAM/SAM/SOM); Linear process map.
* **Decorative Elements:** Breadcrumb tags in the top-left (e.g., "#Решение", "#Конкуренты").

## 7. Information Hierarchy
* **Primary Focal Point:** The center diagram (Process path or Market circles).
* **Secondary Supporting Content:** Categorized text blocks surrounding the diagrams.
* **CTA Prominence:** Not applicable.
* **Visual Scanning Flow:** Process slide: Left-to-right (Timeline); Market slide: Left (Data) to Right (Visual).

## 8. Presentation Style
* **Style:** Technical / Startup-focused / Investor-focused.
* **Feel:** Data-driven, analytical, and process-oriented.

## 9. Unique Observations
* **Wavy Timeline:** The use of a curved, flowing line for a process timeline makes a rigid corporate process feel more organic/modern.
* **Clipped Circles:** The TAM/SAM/SOM circles are clipped/cropped at the top and bottom, suggesting scale beyond the slide boundaries.
* **Tag System:** Using hashtags (e.g., #clients) for section headers gives a modern, digital-first impression.
